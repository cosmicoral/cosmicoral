## 👩‍💻 About Me

Before becoming a software engineer, I spent several years researching sustainability, consumer behaviour, digital platforms and everyday life.

That experience shaped how I think about technology—not just as code, but as something that should fit naturally into people's routines.

Today I build AI-native applications combining modern full-stack engineering, LLMs and product thinking.

Outside of tech, I'm inspired by electronic music, independent music scenes, contemporary design, colourful fashion, exhibitions and multicultural food cultures.

Those experiences quietly influence how I design products.
